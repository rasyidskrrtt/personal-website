About

Who am I?

I am a student of Information Systems at the Faculty of Computer Science at Singaperbangsa University Karawang, currently in my 6th semester. I have a strong passion for Frontend Web Development, with a wealth of experience in this field. One of my most exciting experiences was participating in the Study Independent Program 'MSIB' Full Stack Web Development at Yayasan Adipurna Inovasi Asia (Vocasia).

Before pursuing my degree, I attended 'SMK Travina Prima', where I specialized in 'Teknik Komputer & Jaringan' (TKJ). During this time, I also had the opportunity to participate in an internship at the Youth and Sports Office of Bekasi City (Dipora), specifically in the Active Archive Room section.

Let's collaborate and create impactful web experiences!

As a passionate Frontend Developer, I specialize in creating intuitive and visually appealing web interfaces. With a strong focus on user experience (UX) and performance optimization, I strive to craft responsive, accessible, and interactive web applications. I am continuously exploring the latest web technologies and design trends to deliver solutions that meet both user needs and business objectives. My goal is to build seamless digital experiences that empower users and drive engagement.

Latest News

These are some of the activities I have been involved in during my youth!

Uji Kompetensi Mikrotik

conducted at SMK Travina Prima under PT Bakrie Autopart aims to test students' ability to operate and manage Mikrotik devices. This test is very important as part of the preparation to enter the professional world in the field of computer networks. This program provides an opportunity for students to gain hands-on experience in network configuration and management using Mikrotik devices.

Praktik Kerja Lapangan

Praktik Kerja Lapangan (PKL) is a program designed to give students hands-on experience in the workplace. Through PKL, students can apply their theoretical knowledge in real-world situations, helping them improve their practical skills. The program is also a valuable opportunity for students to learn about the professional environment and understand the challenges in the industry.

Study Independent

Study Independent (Stupen) is a program designed to deepen students' knowledge and skills in a specific field. During the 4 months in the 5th semester at Vocasia, students gain valuable hands-on experience in the industry. Focused on full-stack web development, particularly with the MERN stack, this program equips students with the practical skills needed to thrive in the tech world.

Hiking

I went hiking with my close friend from SMK to Gunung Gede Pangrango, which stands at 2958 meters above sea level. At that time, we were still beginners, and we had an unforgettable experience. We almost froze because the door of our tent broke in the cold, but fortunately, we had an emergency blanket with us. That experience taught us the importance of being prepared for any situation in the mountains.

Photoghrapfer

I was photographing a bus in motion, capturing the moment from above on a pedestrian bridge (JPO). Bus photography is a long-time hobby of mine, and I enjoy the challenge of capturing dynamic movement and the intricate details of the vehicles as they travel. It's fascinating how each bus has its own character, and I love experimenting with angles, lighting, and timing to create unique shots.
